## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign Up** in the top navigation
2. Fill in your details:
   - Username (unique)
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and last name
3. Accept Terms & Conditions
4. Click **Create Account**
5. Check your email for verification (if enabled)

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** in the top navigation
2. Enter your username or email
3. Enter your password
4. (Optional) Check "Remember Me" to stay logged in
5. Click **Login**

### Password Recovery

Forgot your password?

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password
5. Log in with your new password

## Shopping

### Browsing Products

**Homepage:**
- Featured products
- New arrivals
- Spotlight designs
- Trending items

**Shop Page:**
- All products catalog
- Filter by:
  - Category (T-Shirts, Hoodies, etc.)
  - Size (XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL)
  - Color
  - Price range
- Sort by:
  - Newest
  - Price (Low to High)
  - Price (High to Low)
  - Most Popular

### Product Pages

Each product page shows:
- Product images (multiple views)
- Description and details
- Size guide
- Available sizes and colors
- Price
- Add to Cart button
- Customer reviews (if available)

### Adding to Cart

1. Select your size and color
2. Choose quantity
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. Continue shopping or **View Cart**

### Shopping Cart

Your cart shows:
- Product name and image
- Selected size/color
- Price per item
- Quantity (adjustable)
- Subtotal per item
- Cart total

**To modify cart:**
- Change quantity using +/- buttons
- Click trash icon to remove item
- Click **Continue Shopping** to add more
- Click **Proceed to Checkout** when ready

### Checkout Process

#### Step 1: Shipping Information

- Enter or select shipping address
- Provide contact phone number
- Add delivery instructions (optional)

#### Step 2: Review Order

- Verify all items
- Check shipping address
- Review pricing breakdown:
  - Subtotal
  - Tax
  - Shipping
  - Total

#### Step 3: Payment

- Enter payment details (processed securely)
- Review and accept terms
- Click **Place Order**

#### Step 4: Confirmation

- Order confirmation page
- Order number for reference
- Email confirmation sent
- Track your order from account page

## Custom Design Tool

### Accessing the Designer

1. Navigate to **Design Studio** from the menu
2. Or click **Customize** on a product page

### Creating Your Design

#### Choose Your Canvas

- Front view
- Back view
- Full design map

#### Add Elements

**Text:**
- Click **Add Text**
- Type your message
- Choose font, size, color
- Position and rotate as needed

**Graphics:**
- Click **Add Graphics**
- Browse available clipart and icons
- Upload your own images
- Resize and position

**Colors:**
- Click color picker
- Choose from palette or enter hex code
- Apply to text or background

### Design Tools

- **Undo/Redo:** Step backward/forward
- **Layers:** Manage element stacking
- **Alignment:** Center, left, right
- **Grid/Snap:** Enable for precision
- **Zoom:** In/out for details

### Saving Your Design

1. Click **Save Design**
2. Give it a title
3. Choose visibility:
   - **Private:** Only you can see it
   - **Public:** Visible in gallery
4. Click **Save**

### Loading Saved Designs

1. Go to **My Designs** in your account
2. Click on a design to load it
3. Continue editing or add to cart

### Remixing Designs

Love someone's design? Remix it!

1. Find a design in **Spotlight Gallery**
2. Click **Remix This**
3. Design loads with original as base
4. Make your changes
5. Save as your own design

### Ordering Custom Designs

1. Complete your design
2. Click **Add to Cart**
3. Select product, size, color
4. Proceed to checkout

## Orders & Tracking

### Viewing Orders

1. Go to **My Account** > **Orders**
2. See all your orders:
   - Order number
   - Date
   - Status
   - Total amount
3. Click order to view details

### Order Status

- **Pending:** Order received, awaiting processing
- **Processing:** Being prepared for shipment
- **Shipped:** On its way to you
- **Delivered:** Arrived at destination
- **Cancelled:** Order cancelled

### Tracking Shipments

1. Go to order details
2. Find tracking number
3. Click to track with carrier
4. View estimated delivery

### Returns & Exchanges

Need to return something?

1. Go to order details
2. Click **Request Return**
3. Select items and reason
4. Follow instructions
5. Track return status

**Return Policy:**
- 30 days from delivery
- Items must be unworn and tagged
- Original packaging preferred
- Refund or exchange available

## Drop Waitlists

### What are Drops?

Limited-time product releases:
- Exclusive items
- Limited quantities
- Special pricing
- Countdown timers

### Joining a Waitlist

When a drop is announced:

1. Banner appears on homepage
2. Click **Join Waitlist**
3. Enter your email
4. Click **Notify Me**
5. Get confirmation message

### Waitlist Benefits

- Early notification when drop goes live
- Exclusive access window
- Better chance to get sold-out items
- Special offers for waitlist members

### When Drop Goes Live

You'll receive:
- Email notification
- SMS (if enabled)
- In-app notification
- Link to shop the drop

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

- Check username/email spelling
- Verify password (case-sensitive)
- Clear browser cache/cookies
- Try password reset
- Contact support if persists

### Item Not in Stock

- Join waitlist for notifications
- Check back regularly
- Sign up for restock alerts
- Browse similar items

### Design Won't Save

- Check internet connection
- Ensure design has title
- Try refreshing page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support with details

### Cart Issues

- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Log out and back in
- Contact support

### Payment Failed

- Check card details
- Verify billing address
- Try different payment method
- Check with your bank
- Contact support
